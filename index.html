Mimicr: Text Generation using Machine Learning

Input text (more than 100,000 characters) and watch an artificial neural network train itself to follow its form. For example, input a sample of your favorite language and watch Mimicr evolve to communicate. Or input a sample of a famous author's works and see Mimicr learn to replicate the author's style. The possibilities are endless.
